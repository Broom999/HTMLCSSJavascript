Hero Section


Sandvich’s


Sandvich’s is a cozy, pork-centric eatery nestled in the heart of York, OH, where rustic charm meets pig-themed whimsy. From slow-braised belly to flaky Pork Wellington, every dish celebrates the savory soul of swine. With handcrafted comfort food, playful desserts, and a warm, barn-inspired atmosphere, Sandvich’s is where pork lovers come to feast, laugh, and feel right at home.
